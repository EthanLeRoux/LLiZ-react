h2Simple Continuous Tense using [Ya]/h2

div class="text"

h3uVocabulary/u/h3

p

Ngi- /b I r

Ya- /b Is / Are / Am r

Funda- /b to Learn r

Pheka- /b to Cook r

Dlala- /b to Play r

Hamba- /b to Walk r

/p

p

We can create simple sentences in the continuous tense using [Ya]. The [Ya] particle is used to express actions

that are currently happening or ongoing. This can be done by placing [Ya] between the subject prefix and the verb stem.

/p

h3uExamples/u/h3

p

Ngiya funda./b - I am learning. r

Ngiya pheka./b - I am cooking. r

Uyahamba./b - You are walking. r

Bayadlala./b - They are playing. r

/p

h3uStructure/u/h3

p

[Subject Prefix] + [Ya] + [Verb Stem]/b

r

Example:/b "Ngi" (I) + "Ya" + "Funda" (Learn) = Ngiya funda/b (I am learning).

/p
